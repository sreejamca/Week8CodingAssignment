About India

India, officially the Republic of India also commonly known as BhƒÅrat or Hindustan, is a country inSouth Asia. It is the seventh largest country by area. India has been a Federal Republic since 1950, governed through a democratic parliamentary system. It is a pluralistic, multilingual and multi-ethnic society. India has become a fast-growing major economy and a hub for information technology services, with an expanding middle class. Bounded by the Indian Ocean on the south, the Arabian Sea on the southwest, and the Bay of Bengal on the southeast,it shares land borders with to the west Pakistan, China, Nepal , andBhutan to the north; and Bangladesh and Myanmar to the east. In the Indian Ocean, India is in the vicinity of Sri Lanka and the Maldives.

Languages:

According to the Census of India 2001, India has 122 major languages and 1599 other languages. However, figures from other sources vary, primarily due to differences in definition of the terms "language" and "dialect". Article 343 of the Constitution of India stated that the official language of the Union is Hindi in Devanagari script, with official use of English to continue for 15 years from 1947.
